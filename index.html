Minecraft Bingo

How To Play

Create a new world matching the seed of the board with the world. There are a number of ways to play this game, but it all has to do with finishing the task for each box of the bingo board. There are many ways you could find an end goal for this game. You can play until you finish a line of tasks or you could finish the entire board.



Multiplayer



You could play with a friend to figure out who can finish the board or get a line first. Which is why your able to make tasks red as to show they are already finished by opposite player. How you use this board is up to you!
